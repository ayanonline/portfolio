Frequently Asked Questions

What is your development process?

My development process involves understanding requirements, planning, design, development, testing, and deployment. I ensure regular communication throughout the project.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple website might take 2-3 weeks, while more complex applications could take 1-3 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, I offer maintenance and support services to ensure your solution continues to function optimally. We can discuss specific support requirements based on your needs.

What technologies do you specialize in?

I specialize in web development using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Node.js. For AI and ML projects, I work with Python, TensorFlow, and scikit-learn. I also have experience with mobile app development using React Native and Flutter.
