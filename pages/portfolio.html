Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my portfolio and work

What type of projects are in your portfolio?

My portfolio includes a diverse range of projects spanning web development, ESP32/Arduino IoT applications, AI/machine learning experiments, and automation solutions. I showcase both personal projects that demonstrate my technical skills and collaborative work that highlights my ability to work in teams and contribute to larger initiatives.

How do you select projects for your portfolio?

I select projects for my portfolio based on several criteria: technical complexity, problem-solving approach, innovation, real-world applicability, and quality of execution. I aim to include projects that demonstrate my range of skills, showcase my growth as a developer, and highlight my ability to implement effective solutions to diverse challenges.

Do you accept commission or freelance work?

Yes, I'm open to commission and freelance work in web development, IoT projects, and related areas. I enjoy working on new challenges and bringing clients' ideas to life. If you have a project in mind, please contact me through the contact page, and we can discuss your requirements, timeline, and budget to determine if we're a good match.

How often do you update your portfolio?

I update my portfolio regularly as I complete new projects or make significant improvements to existing ones. I typically review and refresh my portfolio every 2-3 months to ensure it accurately represents my current skills and recent work. This way, visitors always see my latest and most relevant projects.
