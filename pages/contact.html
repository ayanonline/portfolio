Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about contacting me and working together

What's the best way to contact you?

The best way to contact me initially is through the contact form on this page. I check these messages daily and will respond within 24-48 hours. For more urgent matters, you can also reach out via email at aysarkar2007@gmail.com or through my social media profiles linked at the bottom of the page.

What information should I include in my message?

To help me provide the most appropriate response, please include: a brief description of your project or inquiry, your timeline if applicable, any specific technologies or requirements, and your preferred method for follow-up communication. The more details you provide, the better I can understand your needs and offer relevant assistance.

Are you available for collaboration or mentorship?

Yes, I'm open to collaboration opportunities with other students, developers, or technology enthusiasts, especially on projects involving IoT, web development, or AI/ML. I'm also happy to share my knowledge and offer mentorship in areas where I have expertise. Please reach out with specific details about what you're looking for.

Do you offer remote services?

Yes, I work remotely with clients and collaborators from anywhere. I use various communication and project management tools to ensure effective remote collaboration. This includes video conferencing, instant messaging, shared documentation, and code repositories. Remote work allows for flexibility and enables me to work with people regardless of geographical location.
